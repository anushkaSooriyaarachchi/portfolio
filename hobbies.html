Since arriving I’ve spent my free time hiking, sightseeing, and getting to know the country one trail, beach, and small town at a time — usually with a camera in hand. The photography started by accident, documenting the solar team through the 2025 race from Darwin to Adelaide, and turned into the way I hold onto the places I’ve been. A few of them are below.
