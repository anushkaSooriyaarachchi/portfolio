Leadership & coordination roles

Secretary — Deakin Ecological Collective

image

As Secretary of the Deakin Ecological Collective I ran the unglamorous engine room — admin, finance tracking, event packages, risk assessments, and grant applications — for events ranging from a handful of people to over 500, like our Everything Aquatic Animal Day that connected Deakin with the wider Geelong community. Day to day it looked like monthly catch-ups, bushwalks, and trips to marine discovery centres, and keeping all of it organised enough to actually happen.

Undergraduate liaison — IEEE Deakin Student Branch

image

I was the undergraduate liaison for Deakin’s IEEE student branch, which mostly meant being the line of communication between the PhD researchers in the intelligent-systems space and undergrads like me — translating between the two, and helping set up and run more than ten branch events along the way.

Committee member — Deakin Engineering Society

image

As a committee member of the Deakin Engineering Society I helped plan and run the events that hold the cohort together — social nights, the World Bee Day trivia, fundraising sausage sizzles, and the annual industry dinner where students get to meet alumni and future employers. My part was usually promotion, setup, and making sure things ran on the night.
